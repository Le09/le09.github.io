Video Games

Shmup Experiments (upcoming)

I am currently working, on and off, on some shmup prototypes. In the meantime, I've done some experimental forks of Kenta Cho's open-source shooters.

rRootage — a boss-rush shmup where you dodge autocreated bullet patterns. This fork adds a "DR" mode inspired by Drainus: you can deploy an absorption sphere to absorb bullets, then reflect them back at enemies on release. Purple bullets can't be absorbed, carve a path with careful meter management.
GitHub

TUMIKI Fighters — a 2D shooter where destroyed enemies break into pieces that stick to your ship, making you progressively more powerful. This fork adds improved screen resizing, a "REV mode" with point-blank damage mechanics, level select and boss rush modes. In REV mode, the ship tilt is fixed unless you press the hide button.
GitHub

JOBifAI

An experimental visual novel where AI dynamically generates both images and narrative text. Bullshit your way through a job interview at the mysterious Grizley company — your success depends on interpreting AI-generated visuals. Every playthrough is different.

Steam · itch.io · GitHub

This is not something you could really do with procedural generation; information is deduced, and reacted to, from the hidden information.

CC's Road to Stardom

A text adventure made for the TALP2022 game jam. Follow CC on his birthday as he pursues his dream of becoming the universe's biggest idol. Input verb-subject commands to explore, collect objects and navigate a small, heartwarming story you can play with your kids. Built with Adventuron.

itch.io · GitHub · Blog: Hackers and painters and pixels

Feathery Christmas

A text adventure made for an Adventuron Christmas Game Jam. Santa needs to save Christmas when all reindeer become unable to move — help him find creative alternatives to deliver gifts. Features both easy and hard difficulty modes. Built with Adventuron.
